A World on the Edge of Darkness

✦

Three crystals once sealed an ancient prison. Now the world has forgotten, the kingdoms turn against one another, and the Prince of Shade stirs in the shadows.

Blue Crystal

Blue belongs to the Humans, who originally came to these lands from the sea.

Green Crystal

Green belongs to the Dwarves, in recognition of their woodland home.

Red Crystal

Red belongs to the Faerkine and their ancient connection to the fabled, long-lost magics of the Elders, also known as the Old Ones, the original bringers of magic to the world.

A preview of Book One

Wyvern

✦

A mother flees through snow-covered mountains while the man she loves buys her time with his life. A child is born into a war that has already begun.

Three thousand years ago, a great evil was defeated. Three crystals sealed its prison, and three races stood united against the coming darkness. But the world has forgotten.

Now the crystals have been found again. In the shadows, the Prince of Shade stirs. Kingdoms fall into war, ancient enemies awaken, and in the dark places of the world something far older prepares to rise.

Brinn Thronso never sought greatness. Raised far from the courts of kings and the schemes of warlords, he is unprepared for the destiny closing around him.
